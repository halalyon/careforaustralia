How It Works

There are 4 simple steps to get started, and we will be here to guide you at every step until you are completely satisfied with everything.

Step 1: Tell Us About What You Want

Tell us a bit about yourself, what you look for in a carer and how we can help you.
Give us a call at 1300 our care (1300 687 2273), email us at connect@careforaustralia.com

Step 2: Choose and Hire Your Carer

After you have told us about your needs and what you look for in a carer, we will recommend a few caresr to you. You can view their profiles and reviews that are written by families that have hired them in the past. You can meet with them before you hire them.

Step 3: Receive Care

The carer will visit your home and provide care to you or your loved ones. You have the choice of setting a schedule that suits you and a rate that is fair for you and the carer before you begin receiving care from the carer.

Step 4: Payment and Write Review

Pay only after you have received care and you may pay by card so that you can have the peace of mind that you or your loved ones do not need to deal with cash. All clients are encouraged to make a review of the carer on the website so that other families are more informed when they hire the carer in the future.
