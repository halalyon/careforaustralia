Pricing

FREE OF CHARGE

It is free for families to be matched with a qualified on our platform.

Once you have found a carer that you like and want to hire, you have the option of setting the rate for the carer. The carer may accept the rate, reject or suggest another rate.

To ensure a fair pay for all carers, families must pay a minimum of $33.40 per hour for personal carers and $50 per hour for registered nurses.

Once you and the carer have agreed on the rate, the carer will visit you or your loved ones at home and provide care.

Most families do not know what a fair rate is to pay the carer so we will also give you information about the average rate for the particular service(s) that you are looking for.
